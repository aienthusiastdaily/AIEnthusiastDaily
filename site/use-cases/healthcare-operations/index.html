Use case

Healthcare operations

AI systems for intake packets, supporting documents, and operational exceptions that should not sit in manual queues forever.

The problem

Healthcare operations teams often receive messy packets, screenshots, PDFs, portal exports, and follow-up documents. Humans still spend too much time opening files, checking fields, and deciding which queue should handle the next step.

What the system does

  • Ingests uploaded packets, forms, letters, or portal exports.
  • Extracts patient, provider, date, policy, and workflow-specific fields.
  • Validates extracted facts against required rules and evidence.
  • Routes clean cases forward and flags exceptions for human review.

Business value

The goal is not to remove all human judgment. The goal is to reduce low-value review, make exceptions clearer, and give teams a more reliable path from incoming documents to operational action.

System shape

Inputs
PDFs, forms, screenshots, portal exports
Outputs
Structured fields, validation status, exception route
Human role
Review edge cases, not every case
