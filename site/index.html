About the practice

The work stays close to the point where a model’s answer becomes someone else’s work.

That means tracing the real workflow, making outputs explicit, testing the edges, and designing a useful handoff when the system is uncertain.

The goal is not autonomy for its own sake. It is a system your team can operate, question, and improve without pretending the hard cases disappeared.
